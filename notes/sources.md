## 1. 학술 논문 / 학술 자료

### 교차문화 아동 놀이
- Edwards, C. P. (2000). "Children's Play in Cross-Cultural Perspective: A New Look at the Six Cultures Study." *Cross-Cultural Research*, 34(4).
  - URL: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/106939710003400402
  - 핵심: 6개 문화권에서 chase, hiding, mock combat 패턴의 보편성 확인

- Gosso, Y., & Carvalho, A. M. A. "Play and Cultural Context." *Encyclopedia on Early Childhood Development*.
  - URL: https://www.child-encyclopedia.com/pdf/expert/play/according-experts/play-and-cultural-context
  - 핵심: 놀이는 모든 사회에서 관찰되는 보편적 특성이나 문화적 맥락에 영향 받음

### 전투놀이 / War Play
- "Play fighting (rough-and-tumble play) in children: developmental and evolutionary perspectives." *Tandfonline*, 2022.
  - URL: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/21594937.2022.2152185
  - 핵심: RTP는 수렵채집 사회 포함 다양한 사회에서 관찰되는 교차문화적 보편 행동

- "Young Children's Play Fighting and Use of War Toys." *Encyclopedia on Early Childhood Development*.
  - URL (ResearchGate): https://www.researchgate.net/publication/290334545
  - 핵심: 전투놀이는 상상력, 사회적 관계, 윤리적 정체성, 문해력 발달에 기여

- Holland, P. (2000). "Take the Toys from the Boys?" *Children & Society*.
  - URL: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.2304/csee.2000.4.2.92

### Brainrot / 디지털 민속
- Owens, E. (2025). "'It speaks to me in brain rot': Theorising 'brain rot' as a genre of participation among teenagers." *New Media & Society* (SAGE).
  - URL: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/14614448251351527
  - 핵심: 브레인롯은 비생산적 메시징을 통한 생산성/자기최적화 압력에 대한 저항

- "Making sense of nonsense: A qualitative investigation of how brainrot content serves generation Z's media needs." *ScienceDirect*.
  - URL: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S294988212600006X
  - 핵심: Gen Z는 미디어 환경의 추출적 구조에서 주체성 회복을 위해 brainrot 소비

- "The Italian Brainrot Phenomenon and the Ethics of Absurdist AI Meme Culture." *Academia.edu*.
  - URL: https://www.academia.edu/130055683/

### 아동 그리기
- "Drawing: The universal language of children." *ResearchGate*.
  - URL: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/323338707
  - 핵심: 그리기는 아동의 보편적 언어

### 참여 문화 / 민속학
